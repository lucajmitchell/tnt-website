About us

TNT are a rock 'n' roll 3-piece band with a classic line up of Timothy on guitar and vocals, Nigel on keys, and Torby on bass.
"As a band, they are versatile, reliable, and very talented."
"So proud of these guys! What a lovely afternoon watching them perform classic hits, plus some new ones."
TNT have been performing together since 2019, building up a varied, high energy set: Oasis, Two Door Cinema Club, Mumford and Sons, classics from Buddy Holly and Elvis Presley, their own originals, and more!
"They take on whatever I throw at them and have developed their own style along the way. Pupils like these are a big reason why I love my job."
"TNT were a dream to work with, and brought a wonderful crowd-pleasing atmosphere."
